Exploring Online Chess Games: A Data Science Based Approach

The project leverages data science techniques to analyze a vast dataset of online chess games, uncovering insights into player strategies and trends. It aims to answer key questions, such as the impact of opening moves on game outcomes, common winning sequences, and the influence of player ratings, with the objectives of improving player performance and enhancing training materials.

SenioCare

SenioCare, a web application, was developed to empower senior citizens by offering a helpline service that allows them to access a range of digital services easily. Administered by SenioCare employees with admin rights, SenioCare helps to bridge the technological gap for seniors, making daily tasks like banking, ordering takeout, and scheduling taxis accessible with a simple phone call.

Deepfake Detection

The rapid progress in machine learning, particularly in deep learning and Generative AI, coupled with the widespread use of social media, have given rise to highly realistic deepfake content, raising concerns about the trustworthiness of video evidence. To address this issue, this project combines key-frame selection, facial feature dropout data augmentation, and a hybrid model using EfficientNet B0 and Swin Transformer, resulting in improved deepfake detection performance while reducing resource consumption.
